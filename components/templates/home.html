About this Guide

Iloilo City, known as the Heart of the Philippines, is a vibrant destination that combines rich history, cultural charm, and modern development. From its centuries-old churches and heritage buildings to its lively markets and flavorful local cuisine, the city offers a unique and welcoming atmosphere for visitors. Exploring Iloilo is also easy with its public transportation system. Jeepneys, taxis, and ride-hailing services are readily available, making it simple to get around and experience everything the city has to offer.

This guide provides jeepney route information to help both locals and tourists in going around the city.
